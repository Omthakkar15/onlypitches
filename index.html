How Our Website Works —
Step by Step

1
Landing on the Website

Discover Our Services

Client visits VisaPath.com and lands on the Home Page. They browse visa types, read success stories, check country-specific guides in the Blog/News section, and understand what we offer — all without creating an account.

Home Page Services Page Blog & News

Getting in Touch

Book a Free Consultation

Client clicks "Free Consultation" and fills the Contact Form — name, email, WhatsApp, destination country, and visa type. Our team receives it instantly via email and responds within 2 hours on WhatsApp or call.

Contact Form WhatsApp Integration Email Notification

Document Checklist

Get Your Personalized Plan

After the consultation call, our expert sends a custom document checklist. Client uploads documents directly through a secure section on the website. We review and flag any missing items instantly.

Application Filing

Our consultants prepare and submit the complete application to the embassy or immigration authority. You get a tracking ID and live status updates right on your dashboard — no need to follow up manually.

Live Tracking Status Emails Embassy Liaison

Once the visa is approved, client receives a notification and can download their summary report. We invite them to leave a Review on our website which helps other applicants feel confident. Journey complete!
