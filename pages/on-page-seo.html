On-Page SEO — The Checklist That Gets You Found on Google

You built a website. Now Google needs to understand it. Here's every on-page element that matters — written for business owners in India.

India · 2026 Guide

What is on-page SEO?

On-page SEO is everything inside your website that tells Google what a page is about. Think of it like labelling boxes before a move — clear labels mean things end up in the right place. This on-page SEO guide covers every element you can control directly.

Unlike off-page SEO (backlinks, social signals), on-page SEO is entirely in your hands. For a local business in Mumbai, Delhi, Ahmedabad, or anywhere in India — it's often the fastest ranking win you'll get.

Complete on-page SEO checklist

Work through this list page by page. You don't need perfection on day one — but every item you skip is a ranking signal left on the table.

  • Content Quality — Write for humans first. Answer real questions customers ask. Thin, copy-pasted content won't rank in 2026.
  • Meta Title & Description — Your SERP headline and snippet. Keep titles under 60 characters, descriptions under 155. Include your city or India where natural.
  • URL Optimisation — Short, readable URLs like /pages/why-website-for-business.html beat messy ones with IDs.
  • Heading Tags — One H1 per page. H2 for sections, H3 for sub-points. Use italic and bold styling for readability, not keyword stuffing.
  • Canonical Tag — Tells Google which URL is the original. Prevents your own pages competing against each other.
  • Internal & External Links — Link between your pages and to trusted external sources. Both build context and trust.
  • Image Optimisation — Compress images, add descriptive alt text, use WebP where possible. A 4MB hero image kills speed scores.
  • XML Sitemap — A roadmap for Google crawlers. Submit via Search Console. Ours: XML sitemap file.
  • Robots.txt — Tells crawlers what to index. Ours: robots configuration file.
  • 301 Redirections — When you change a URL, redirect the old one. Otherwise you lose built-up SEO equity.
  • Custom 404 Page — A friendly error page with links home beats a blank screen that loses visitors forever.
  • Schema Markup — JSON-LD structured data helps Google show rich results — ratings, FAQs, business hours.
  • Open Graph & Twitter Cards — Controls how links look when shared on WhatsApp, Facebook, and LinkedIn.
  • Page Speed — Google tracks Core Web Vitals. Compress assets, lazy-load images, avoid bloated scripts.
  • Mobile Friendliness — Over 80% of Indian users browse on mobile. Broken mobile layouts hurt rankings.
  • Fix Broken Links — Monthly checks. Dead links erode trust with users and search engines alike.

Meta tags — your first impression on Google

When someone searches "best salon website India" or "restaurant website builder," your meta title is the blue link they click. Your meta description is the grey text below.

Pro tip: Write your meta description like a WhatsApp message you'd actually send — clear, specific, with a reason to click. "Premium templates for 55+ Indian business types. Browse free. Chat on WhatsApp." beats "Welcome to our homepage."

Heading structure — don't skip the hierarchy

Every page needs exactly one H1 — your main topic. Break content into H2 sections. Use italic for emphasis and bold styling where it helps readers scan faster.

Google doesn't rank bold vs italic directly — but well-structured headings keep people reading longer. And dwell time is a real signal.

Quick wins for Indian businesses this week

  1. Add a unique meta title and description to your homepage
  2. Submit your sitemap in Google Search Console
  3. Check mobile view on your phone — fix anything that clips
  4. Add alt text to every image
  5. Read why a website is required for your business before investing in SEO
