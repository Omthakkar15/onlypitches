About LabCore

25 Years of Diagnostic Excellence

LabCore Diagnostics has been a trusted partner in healthcare since 1999, delivering accurate and timely diagnostic results that empower physicians to make informed clinical decisions.

Our NABL-accredited facility is equipped with the latest analyzers and staffed by qualified pathologists, microbiologists, and lab technicians committed to the highest standards of quality.

  • NABL & ISO 15189 Accredited Facility

How It Works

Step by Step

From booking your test to receiving certified results — here's exactly what happens at every stage of your LabCore experience.

1
Landing on the website

Discover Our Services

Patient visits LabCore.in and lands on the Home Page. They browse available diagnostic tests, read about our NABL accreditations, check the test menu, and explore health packages — all without creating an account.

Home PageTest MenuBlog & Guides
2
Getting in touch

Book a Test or Appointment

Patient clicks "Book a Test" and fills the Booking Form — name, mobile, email, test required, and preferred date. Our team receives the request instantly and confirms within 2 hours via WhatsApp or call.

Booking FormWhatsApp ConfirmHome Collection
3
Sample collection

Sample Collected & Processed

A trained phlebotomist arrives at the patient's doorstep or the patient walks into a collection centre. Sample is collected, labelled with a unique tracking ID, and transported to the NABL-certified main lab under cold chain protocols.

Home PhlebotomyUnique Sample IDCold Chain
4
Lab analysis

Testing & Quality Verification

Samples are analysed on automated analysers by qualified pathologists. Every result passes a dual-verification quality check before approval. STAT and urgent tests are fast-tracked with priority processing — no manual follow-up needed.

Automated AnalysersPathologist ReviewSTAT Priority
5
Report delivered 🎉

Receive & Share Your Report

Once verified, the patient receives a certified digital report via WhatsApp, Email, and the Patient App. Reports can be shared directly with their doctor. Hardcopy is available on request at any collection centre. Journey complete!

WhatsApp ReportEmail PDFPatient AppDownload Anytime
